About

From my very first position at Republic Pictures as a marketing assistant, through a stream of magical posts, assisting, supporting and learning from the most brilliant minds in the towers, on the sets, through production and marketing, I’ve been led on an inalterable path to this...my life’s dream. Producing entertainment for the screen. Wiler Productions opened its doors in January 2009.

On the heels of a year filled with box office hits derived from graphic novels, we’ve optioned two of the most exciting and most visible graphic story properties available: the original heavy metal master of sin, Death Fest '74 and legendary comic book creator Bill DuBay's The Rook. We’ve just begun to develop these electrifying properties for the silver screen. Watch us grow. We’ve got a lot more fun up our sleeves.
